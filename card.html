Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers instinctively love to swim.They're always up for a game of fetch in the lake.

  • Scientific Name: canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 60 centimeters
  • Average Life Span: 11 years
  • Habitat: cold temperature

The Golden Retriever is a sturdy, muscular dog of medium size,broad head, with its friendly and intelligent eyes, short ears, and straight muzzle, is a breed hallmark.
